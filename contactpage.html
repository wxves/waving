Server issues

Contact us by filling out the form below when experiencing issues related to our server, if online, always ask a staff member before sending a help-form. A response may take up to a few hours or days in some cases, please be patient.

Discord problems

Contact us by filling out the form below when you are experiencing any discord-related issues. A response may take up to a few hours or days in some cases, please be patient.

Payment issues

Contact us by filling out the form below when you are experiencing any payment or billing issues. Payment issues have priority over other forms, so expect a response in a few hours or less. Please be patient.

Staff applications

Apply for staff by filling out the form below. Please note that your applications may be rejected by our current manager/staff. If so, please do not apply again for another month, otherwise, you will be given an application ban. Please allow up to a few days for our staff managers to review your application. Note: do NOT spam our staff asking them to review your application faster because doing so will also result in an application ban.

Your application is now complete. It will be reviewed by the Staff Manager and other Senior Staff Members, if accepted you will be contacted and receive some training to make sure you know what to do!


If your application is denied, please wait a minimum of 1 month to apply again. Understand that not all applications will be denied due to their quality (or lack of), they will also be reviewed on our need for staff.


If your application takes longer than 1 week to be reviewed, please contact the Current Staff Manager majorkarl (IGN) Or major#0001 (discord).


Your application will only be reviewed by the Staff Manager, or in some circumstances the Owner/Co-Owner. Please note that if you message the staff directly regarding your application, it will be immediately denied, regardless of quality. All applications must come through this form.


Thank you for applying, and good luck!
